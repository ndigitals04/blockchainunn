Founders

Blockchain UNN is a community of digital enthusiasts with a drive to educate, impact and develop members of the University Community with both basic and advanced knowledge of cryptocurrency and blockchain, as well as it's technology and development in UNN/UNEC Campuses.

BlockchainUNN was founded April 27, 2021 by:

  • Nduka Annaelechukwu Anthony
  • Okoye Kelvin Chibuoyim
  • Alvan Okechukwu Victor

on a basis to ignite the adoption of Blockchain within the school communty.
